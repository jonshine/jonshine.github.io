ABOUT

Data science is about using the power of computing and statistics to solve real world problems. Big data can be a big problem unless you have the right statistical tools to take complicated systems and distill them down to the few key variables that matter.

I have 10 years of experience working with data in finance, economics, and medicine so I know good analysis requires collaboration from start to finish. I enjoy sharing my knowledge of data science and econometrics so feel free to contact me with projects and questions. I am available for hire.
